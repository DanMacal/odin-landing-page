Choose Your Fitness Journey: Explore Our Membership Tiers

Basic

Entry-level membership suitable for individuals looking for standard access to gym facilities.

    Features:
  • Unlimited access to gym equipment during regular hours
  • Access to basic fitness classes.
  • General use of amenities likelocker rooms and showers.

Premium

An upgraded membership offering additional perks for those seeking a more comprehensive gym experience.

    Features:
  • Unlimited 24/7 gym access.
  • Access to a wider variety of fitness classes, including specialty classes.
  • Personalized workout plans or consultations.
  • Discounts on merchandise and additional services.

VIP

A top-tier membership with exclusive benefits and personalized services.

    Features:
  • All features of Premium Membership.
  • Priority access to special events or classes.
  • Dedicated personal trainer sessions.
  • Exclusive access to premium amenities (sauna, spa, etc.).
  • Special discounts or partnerships with local businesses.

Family

Ideal for families or couples looking to work out together.

    Features:
  • All benefits of Basic or Premium Membership for multiple family members.
  • Group fitness classes suitable for various ages and fitness levels.
  • Family-friendly events or classes.
  • Flexible billing options for joint memberships.
